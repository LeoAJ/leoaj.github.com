Top Friends FaceBook App

JavaScript

Go and check out who is your Top 10 closest friends on FaceBook

This is a application that once you login to your FaceBook account, I can tell you which of your friends did follow and look on your personal wall recently.

To rank your friends, I came up a algorithm based on your friends behavior on your personal. It is a way to transfer human behavior to computer science for more analysis. Behind the scene, it is not only the software, but more business values inside.

  1. Post on your wall
  2. Comment on your post
  3. Click like on your post

There are also the ranking of eaching operation, post on your wall will get the highest score and then make comment, then click like. Based on this algorithm, each friends will get a score based on what he or she has done on your personal wall. And that is how the ranking comes from.

The most interesting function I built is gender ratio. It is obviously that we all want to how many girls I knew V.S. boys.

Twitter Search

JavaScript

Simple twitter search jQuery plugin

Search any query word in Twiiter, limit search results and refresh results in specific period of time. To build as a jQuery plugin, it is easy to use and easy to extend for future usage.

Usage:
  1. Search term: You can type any query word you want to search in Twitter.
  2. Limitation: Limit how many results return.
  3. Frequency: How many ms reload the app again, for instance, 1000ms = 1s. leave text box empty to disable this function.
